Omelette Recipe

Description

Perfecting your omelette making is a great way to develop your cooking skills because it teaches you all about temperature, timing and seasoning. Plus, you can have a delicious meal in a matter of minutes!

Ingredients

  • Spray oil
  • 2 eggs
  • 2 tablespoons skim milk or water
  • Dash salt and pepper
  • Filling of your choice

Steps

  1. A frying pan with oil and set over a medium-high heat. In a bowl lightly beat eggs, milk, salt and pepper together, then pour mixture into pan. Pull mixture away from edges of pan using a spatula and let uncooked egg flow into the space. Do this until egg is just cooked through.
  2. Add filling of your choice to one half of the omelette. Fold over the other half using the spatula. Cook for a few more minutes, until filling is warmed through. Gently slide omelette onto plate to serve.
